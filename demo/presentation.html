The Problem

60%

Duplicate Work

Team members redo analyses that someone else already completed. No shared memory of what's been done, what queries were written, or what was found.

0

Knowledge Capture

Insights from AI conversations vanish after each session. Claude helps you find an answer — then it's gone. The next person starts from scratch.

No Quality Gate

Bad Data Spreads

Vague claims ("SLA improved"), subjective opinions, and stale findings get shared as facts. No review process, no expiry, no accountability.

Siloed

Teams Don't Connect

Data team finds a pattern. Ops team doesn't know. Product makes decisions without it. Each team's knowledge stays locked in their own conversations.
